About Us

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest organization of technical professionals with more than 400,000 members around the world contributing towards advancing technological innovation. Members of IEEE inspire people all over the world through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards and various other professional and educational activities.

Our Mission & Vision

IEEE's core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity. IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions. IEEE - IIT The executive committee and the members at the IEEE student branch of IIT are very energetic and passionate towards improving as well as innovating new projects to make an impact to the society. As a recently formed student branch the number of events and other activities organized by the IEEE IIT is highly recognized by others. Hope to see more activities in the coming years and align towards the IEEE global motives.

Our Aim

Our aim is to promote creativity and team work towards Information Technology.

Our Milestone

The student branch has organized the immensely successful IEEE programming Challenge hosted during the annual IIT Cutting Edge exhibition for the students of National and international secondary schools in the country. The success of the event can be seen by the participation of schools from around the country. Being dedicated to the advancement of technological concepts and methodologies even within the Institute itself, the IEEE Student Branch hosts a monthly workshop conducted by reputed individuals in the technological arena for the undergraduate students inside IIT – thus successfully propagating and advancing technological excellence both inside the Institute as well as to its surrounding entities. We are proud to say that IEEE Student Branch of IIT were the first to organize an Inter University hackathon name ‘Code Sprint ’in Sri Lanka. Also we are proud to say that IEEE Student Branch of IIT were the first to organize an Inter University startup battle name ‘Code Sprint ’in Sri Lanka.
